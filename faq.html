Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DOES IT WORK?

You fill out the order form telling us exactly what you want, we send the message to one of our runners, we will contact you to confirm everything, and bring whatever you would like...it’s that easy!

WHAT CAN I HAVE DELIVERED?

Runner will deliver anything that is sold within our delivery area. We deliver from your favorite restaurants, stores, dry cleaners, hardware stores, coffee shops, ect.

WHAT CAN'T I HAVE DELIVERED?

We cannot deliver any chemicals, explosives, or other possibly harmful products.  

We cannot deliver anything that requires identification for the purchase.  

We cannot deliver any prescription medications.

DOES THE PRODUCT PRICE CHANGE?

We add our small networking fee and delivery fee at the end of the order and send you an itemized receipt!

HOW MUCH DO YOU CHARGE FOR DELIVERY?

We charge between 5-10% of your total order price for our networking services and processing fees and a minimum 2-5$ tip for your driver, depending on the distance. We strongly encourage tipping your Runners more as well. 100% of the tips go directly to them and keep them happy!

HOW DO I PAY?

Runner, encourages cashless transactions as much as possible. We use SQUARE Register POS to ensure the safety of our customers. The square system is highly secure, very well known, and highly trusted. (Any orders over $40 must be paid with card)

We also accept cash! Just let us know first, and we can make it work!

WHY SHOULD I SIGN UP?

We like to encourage all of our repeat customers to sign up for an account so we know how often you use our services. We will have deals for our frequent customers soon!

WHAT IF I AM OUTSIDE OF THE DELIVERY RANGE?

Give us a call! Let's see if we can work something out!
